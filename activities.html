Teaching & Supervision

I use a student-centered approach built on reflective learning, peer instruction, and hands-on demonstrations. I treat the classroom like a research lab where students ask questions, form hypotheses, and learn by doing. I supervise BA and MSc theses in gaze perception, emotion processing, EEG methodology, and social exclusion paradigms.
