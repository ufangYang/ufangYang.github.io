Cognitive & computational neuroscience · Freie Universität Berlin

I investigate how faces become meaningful — how the brain reads identity, expression, and social cues from a single glance. My tools are EEG, eye-tracking, and multi-lab open-science methods. I care about reproducibility and analytic discipline as much as about the questions themselves.
