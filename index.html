About

My group investigates gaze direction, emotional expression processing, and social exclusion at the neural level, using EEG, eye-tracking, and computational modeling.

Beyond the lab, I co-chair SIPS, chair the ARTEM-IS international EEG reporting standard, and coordinate EEGManyPipelines.
