About

Cognitive neuroscientist, open science advocate, researcher at Freie Universität Berlin.

I study how humans decode social signals from faces, and I work to make neuroscience more open, reproducible, and inclusive. My path has taken me from Taiwan to France, Belgium, and Germany.

Open Science & Beyond

I believe the research culture ECRs inherit doesn't have to be the one we perpetuate. I co-lead ARTEM-IS and EEGManyPipelines, big team science initiatives that bring researchers together to improve methodology, transparency, and reproducibility.
